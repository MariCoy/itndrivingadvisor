Refund Policy

We stand behind our service with a 100% money-back guarantee. Learn about our refund policy and how to request a refund.

Last updated: January 15, 2025

100% Money-Back Guarantee

We're so confident in our service that we offer a complete money-back guarantee. If your IDP is not accepted where it should be valid, we'll refund your money in full.

When You Can Get a Refund

We offer full refunds in the following situations:

IDP Not Accepted

If your IDP is rejected by authorities, car rental agencies, or other institutions where it should be legally valid according to international conventions.

Processing Error

If we are unable to process your application due to an error on our part or technical issues with our systems.

Unused IDP

If you request a refund within 30 days of purchase and have not used your IDP for any driving activities abroad.

Service Failure

If we fail to deliver your IDP within the promised timeframe due to circumstances within our control.

Non-Refundable Situations

While we offer generous refund terms, there are some situations where refunds cannot be provided:

Customer-Related Issues

  • • Your domestic driver's license expires or becomes invalid
  • • You provide incorrect, false, or misleading information in your application
  • • You fail to carry both your IDP and original license while driving
  • • You violate local traffic laws or driving regulations
  • • You use your IDP for purposes other than driving (e.g., as general identification)

External Factors

  • • Changes in local laws or regulations after your IDP was issued
  • • Political situations or diplomatic issues affecting IDP recognition
  • • Individual company policies that exceed legal requirements
  • • Emergency situations or temporary restrictions in destination countries
  • • Misunderstandings due to language barriers (when proper translation is provided)

Service Completion

  • • Refund requests made more than 30 days after purchase
  • • IDPs that have been successfully used for driving abroad
  • • Physical IDPs that have been shipped and delivered successfully
  • • Requests based on change of travel plans (unless within 30-day window)

Partial Refunds

In some cases, we may offer partial refunds:

Shipping and Handling

For physical IDP orders, shipping and handling fees are generally non-refundable unless:

  • • The package was lost or damaged during shipping
  • • We made an error in processing your order
  • • The delivery was significantly delayed due to our error

Express Processing Fees

Express processing fees may be refunded if we fail to meet the expedited delivery timeframe due to our error.

Your Consumer Rights

This refund policy is in addition to your statutory consumer rights, which may vary depending on your location:

EU Consumers

Under EU consumer protection laws, you have the right to cancel your order within 14 days of purchase without giving a reason.

This right may be limited for digital products that have been delivered.

US Consumers

Consumer protection laws vary by state. You may have additional rights under your state's consumer protection statutes.

Contact your state's consumer protection agency for more information.

Our refund policy does not limit your statutory rights as a consumer. If you believe your consumer rights have been violated, please contact us or your local consumer protection authority.

Refund FAQ

Common questions about our refund process and policies.

How long do refunds take to process?

Refunds are typically processed within 5-10 business days to your original payment method. The exact time depends on your bank or payment provider.

Can I get a refund if I change my travel plans?

Yes, if you request a refund within 30 days of purchase and haven't used your IDP yet. After 30 days or if the IDP has been used, refunds are not available for change of plans.

What if my IDP is rejected by a car rental company?

If your IDP is rejected where it should be legally accepted, contact us immediately. We'll investigate the situation and provide a full refund if the rejection was unjustified.

Do you refund shipping costs?

Shipping costs are refunded only if the issue was caused by our error, such as processing delays or shipping to the wrong address due to our mistake.

Can I get a refund to a different payment method?

For security reasons, refunds are typically processed to the original payment method. In special circumstances, we may accommodate alternative refund methods with proper verification.
