Cookie Policy

How we use cookies to improve your experience

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us improve your browsing experience, analyze site traffic, and provide secure and personalized services.

How We Use Cookies

International Driving Advisor uses cookies to:

  • Remember your preferences and settings.
  • Enable secure login and checkout.
  • Analyze site traffic and performance (e.g., pages visited, time on site).
  • Support marketing and advertising that may be relevant to you.

Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

Required for website operation (e.g., login, checkout).

Performance Cookies

Collect anonymous data on how visitors use our site.

Functional Cookies

Remember your choices and personalize your experience.

Advertising Cookies

Used to deliver relevant ads through trusted third parties (e.g., Google).

Third-Party Cookies

We may allow trusted third parties (such as Google Analytics or advertising partners) to set cookies to help us understand user behavior and deliver ads. These providers may collect information such as IP address, device type, and browsing activity.

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies by adjusting your browser settings:

  • Accept all cookies
  • Block all cookies
