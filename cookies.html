Cookie Policy

Learn about how we use cookies and similar technologies to improve your experience on our website.

Last updated: January 15, 2025

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide information to website owners about how users interact with their sites.

This Cookie Policy explains how International Driving Advisor ("we," "our," or "us") uses cookies and similar technologies on our website. By continuing to use our website, you consent to our use of cookies as described in this policy.

This policy should be read alongside our Privacy Policy, which provides more information about how we collect, use, and protect your personal data.

Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly and cannot be disabled. They are usually set in response to actions you take, such as setting privacy preferences, logging in, or filling out forms.

Examples of Essential Cookies:

  • • Session management cookies
  • • Security cookies
  • • Load balancing cookies
  • • Cookie consent preferences

Performance and Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting and reporting information anonymously. This helps us improve our website's performance and user experience.

What These Cookies Do:

  • • Count website visits and traffic sources
  • • Measure page load times and performance
  • • Identify popular content and pages
  • • Track user navigation patterns
  • • Help us identify and fix technical issues

Functional Cookies

These cookies enable enhanced functionality and personalization. They may be set by us or by third-party providers whose services we use on our website.

Functional Features Include:

  • • Remembering your preferences and settings
  • • Providing live chat support
  • • Enabling social media features
  • • Customizing content based on your location
  • • Saving form data between sessions

Marketing and Advertising Cookies

These cookies are used to deliver relevant advertisements and marketing content. They track your browsing activity across websites to build a profile of your interests.

Marketing Cookie Functions:

  • • Show relevant advertisements
  • • Limit the number of times you see an ad
  • • Measure advertising campaign effectiveness
  • • Enable retargeting campaigns
  • • Provide personalized content recommendations

Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies on our website are set by third-party services that we use to enhance your experience. These services have their own privacy policies and cookie practices.

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our website. This service uses cookies to collect information about your visit, including pages viewed, time spent on the site, and how you arrived at our website.

Learn more: You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Payment Processors

When you make a payment, our payment processors (such as Stripe or PayPal) may set cookies to ensure secure transaction processing and fraud prevention.

Customer Support Tools

We use third-party customer support tools that may set cookies to provide live chat functionality and track support interactions.

Social Media Platforms

If you interact with social media features on our website (such as sharing buttons), the respective social media platforms may set cookies to track these interactions.

How Long Do Cookies Last?

Cookies have different lifespans depending on their purpose:

Session Cookies

These are temporary cookies that are deleted when you close your browser. They help maintain your session while you navigate our website.

Duration: Until browser is closed

Persistent Cookies

These cookies remain on your device for a set period or until you delete them. They remember your preferences across multiple visits.

Duration: 30 days to 2 years (varies by purpose)

Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You have several options for managing cookies on our website:

Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can:

  • • Block all cookies
  • • Block third-party cookies only
  • • Delete existing cookies
  • • Set up notifications when cookies are being set

Important: Blocking or deleting cookies may affect your ability to use certain features of our website, including the application process for your International Driving Permit.

Browser-Specific Instructions

Chrome

Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies and other site data

Firefox

Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data

Safari

Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data

Edge

Settings → Cookies and site permissions → Cookies and site data

Opt-Out Tools

You can also use these industry tools to opt out of certain types of tracking:

  • • Digital Advertising Alliance Opt-Out
  • • Network Advertising Initiative Opt-Out
  • • Your Online Choices (EU)

Mobile Devices and App Tracking

If you access our website through a mobile device, you can manage tracking preferences through your device settings:

iOS Devices

Go to Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking to manage app tracking permissions.

You can also limit ad tracking in Settings → Privacy & Security → Apple Advertising.

Android Devices

Go to Settings → Privacy → Ads to opt out of personalized advertising.

You can also reset your advertising ID to limit tracking across apps.

Do Not Track Signals

Some browsers include a "Do Not Track" feature that sends a signal to websites requesting that they not track your browsing activity. Currently, there is no industry standard for how websites should respond to these signals.

We do not currently respond to Do Not Track signals, but we provide you with various options to control cookie usage as described in this policy. We will continue to monitor developments in this area and may update our practices as standards evolve.

  • • Notify you through our website or via email

  • • Give you the opportunity to review and accept the updated policy

Your continued use of our website after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated Cookie Policy.
