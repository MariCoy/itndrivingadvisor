About Us

Making international travel easier, one translation at a time

At International Driving Advisor, our mission is simple: to make international travel easier by providing drivers with an easy-to-read, multilingual translation of their domestic driver's license.

We understand that when traveling abroad, language barriers can make it difficult to communicate with rental agencies, hotels, or traffic authorities. That's why we created a reliable service that helps travelers present their valid driver's license in multiple languages for easier understanding.

Who We Are

  • A private, independent service provider.
  • Not affiliated with any government agency, embassy, consulate, or the American Automobile Association (AAA).
  • A team committed to delivering fast, secure, and professional document translations to help drivers feel more confident when traveling abroad.

What We Don't Do

  • We cannot guarantee acceptance by every authority — acceptance is always at the discretion of third parties.
