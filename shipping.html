Shipping FAQ

Common questions about shipping and delivery of your International Driving Permit.

Can I change my shipping address after ordering?

Yes, if you contact us within 24 hours of placing your order. Once the package has shipped, address changes may not be possible and could incur additional fees.

Do you ship to P.O. Boxes?

We can ship to P.O. Boxes using standard shipping only. Express and overnight shipping require a physical address for signature confirmation.

What if I'm not home for delivery?

Most carriers will attempt delivery 2-3 times and leave a notice. You can usually arrange pickup at a local facility or reschedule delivery online.

Can I ship to a different country than my license?

Yes, you can ship your IDP to any supported country. This is common for travelers who are already abroad or planning extended trips.

Is my package insured?

Yes, all shipments include full insurance coverage. If your package is lost or damaged, we'll replace it at no charge and file the insurance claim.

What's included in the physical package?

Your package includes an official IDP booklet, a plastic ID card, and a protective sleeve. Digital access is also included at no extra charge.
